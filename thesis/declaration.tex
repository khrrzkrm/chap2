\section*{Declaration}

\paragraph{Use of Generative Artificial Intelligence Tools}

Generative artificial intelligence tools were used during the preparation of this document for writing support and editorial assistance. Specifically, OpenAI ChatGPT, Google Gemini, and Grammarly up to their last versions (January 2026) were used notably for
(i) \LaTeX~programming assistance and (ii) language polishing, paraphrasing, and refining the readability and logical flow of the text. The generated output was critically verified and revised by the author, who assumes full responsibility for the scientific content.
 \\No personal data, confidential information, or third-party copyright-protected materials were provided to these systems unless explicitly permitted.\\ This disclosure follows the guidance from ``Deutsche Forschungsgemeinschaft''\footnote{\url{https://www.dfg.de/en/news/news-topics/announcements-proposals/2023/info-wissenschaft-23-72}} (DFG) and the University of Lübeck's\footnote{\url{https://www.uni-luebeck.de/fileadmin/uzl_ki-uzl/pdf/20250525_KILeitlinieUzL_Web.pdf}} on the reporting of generative-model use in research outputs.
